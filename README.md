Focusly - Your Personal Study Tracker
Focusly is a smart and minimalistic study tracker app built to help students stay consistent, organized, and motivated in their daily learning journey. Track your study sessions, manage tasks, view analytics, and build strong study habits with streaks and achievements. 

Features:
- Plan and Track Study Sessions: Set daily, weekly or custom study goals and track total study time and session history.
- Subject and Task Management: Add subjects, topics, and to-do items.
- Progress Analytics: Time breakdown, Daily/Weekly Stats and clean charts and visual insights to monitor productivity.
- Achievements and Streaks: Track consistency streaks and earn motivational badges for milestones.
- Reminders and Notifications: Set study reminders and get notified to stay on schedule.

Tech Stack:
- Language: Kotlin
- IDE: Android Studio
- UI: XML Layouts
